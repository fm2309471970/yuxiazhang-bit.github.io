Hi! I am currently a Tenured Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology at Beijing Institute of Technology, China. I received my Ph.D degree in July 2020 from School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science (EECS), Peking University, China. I was very fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Minghui Zhou and Prof. Zhi Jin .

My research primarily focuses on improving the quality and efficiency of software development through empirical studies to build and identify new theories and applying big data mining, deep learning, and LLMs to develop intelligent support tools. We are also interested in improving the sustainability of open source software, under which I am broadly interested in improving our understanding of companies' engagement with open source software projects. My research has been published in top-tier conferences and journals, including TSE, TOSEM, ICSE, ESEC/FSE, and ASE, and received two ACM SIGSOFT distinguished paper awards, i.e., ICSE 2022 and ESEC/FSE 2023.

Our group is actively recruiting self-motivated undergraduate/master/PhD students interested in AI for software development, open source software, or empirical software engineering. Don't hesitate to contact me if you are interested in working with us.
